# Whisper Crystals — Faction Registry

## Core Factions

### Felid Corsairs (Cats — Player Faction)

- **ID:** `felid_corsairs`
- **Realm:** Realm of the Feline Courts
- **Species:** Cat
- **Ideology:** Freedom through power
- **Alignment:** Chaotic Independent
- **Government:** Decentralised Captains
- **Politics:** Meritocracy — leadership earned, not inherited
- **Culture:** Crew loyalty earned through loot and leadership, not rank. Fiercely self-determined.
- **Ships:** Fast, low armour, high manoeuvrability. Asymmetric, jury-rigged, devastatingly effective.
- **Special:** Sole producers of Whisper Crystals. This is their power and their target.
- **Abilities:** Stealth, speed, cunning diplomacy
  - **Crystal Refining** — only faction that can refine raw crystals
  - **Shadow Running** — evade detection in hostile territory (-30% ambush rate)
  - **Cunning Diplomacy** — unlocks bluff/misdirect dialogue options
- **Base Ship Stats:** Speed 8 | Armour 3 | Firepower 5 | Crystal Capacity 6 | Crew 4
- **Key NPCs:** Aristotle (leader), Death (rival)

### Canis League (Dogs)

- **ID:** `canis_league`
- **Realm:** Realm of the Canine Order
- **Species:** Dog
- **Ideology:** Order through loyalty
- **Alignment:** Lawful Hierarchical
- **Government:** Military Command
- **Politics:** Military hierarchy and pack loyalty
- **Culture:** Loyalty, honour, hierarchy. They follow orders. Naval power structure.
- **Ships:** Bulkier, stronger armour, more guns. Organised fleet formations.
- **Special:** Completely crystal-dependent. This drives their aggression.
- **Abilities:** Organised warfare, siege tactics, superior logistics
  - **Organised Warfare** — fleet formations grant +15% armour when 2+ ships present
  - **Siege Tactics** — can blockade supply routes (-50% crystal throughput)
  - **Superior Logistics** — resupply/repair at League ports costs 20% less
- **Base Ship Stats:** Speed 4 | Armour 8 | Firepower 7 | Crystal Capacity 5 | Crew 8
- **Key NPCs:** Dave (commander)

### The Lions (Noble Cat Hierarchy)

- **ID:** `lions`
- **Realm:** Realm of the Feline Courts (noble branch)
- **Species:** Cat (Noble)
- **Ideology:** Rule by divine birthright
- **Alignment:** Lawful Aristocratic
- **Government:** Noble Hierarchy
- **Politics:** They were rulers before crystals existed and intend to rule after. Aristotle is an insult to their lineage.
- **Culture:** Self-declared noblest beasts of the jungle. Arrogant, political, claim divine right.
- **Ships:** Ornate, balanced stats with political modifications.
- **Special:** Claim birthright over Whisper Crystals. Pressure Aristotle from within cat hierarchy.
- **Abilities:** Diplomatic pressure, noble authority, royal decree
  - **Diplomatic Pressure** — force tribute demands costing crystals if refused
  - **Noble Authority** — +20% political influence over neutral factions in their regions
  - **Royal Decree** — can temporarily close trade routes to hostile factions
- **Base Ship Stats:** Speed 5 | Armour 6 | Firepower 5 | Crystal Capacity 7 | Crew 6
- **Tension:** Need Aristotle's knowledge but resent owing anything to a street cat.

### The Wolves (Military Elite Dogs)

- **ID:** `wolves`
- **Realm:** Realm of the Canine Order (military elite branch)
- **Species:** Wolf
- **Ideology:** Dominance through superior tactics and evolutionary right
- **Alignment:** Lawful Dominant
- **Government:** Military Stratocracy
- **Politics:** Self-declared noblest beasts of the forest. They do not follow orders — they give them.
- **Culture:** Strategic, disciplined, utterly convinced of superiority. Dominance and calculated power.
- **Ships:** Tactical, optimised for combat. The toughest opponents in space.
- **Special:** Want to break the crystal monopoly by force. The strategic mind behind Canis League military ambition.
- **Abilities:** Tactical superiority, pack coordination, evolutionary right
  - **Tactical Superiority** — +20% firepower when initiating combat (first-strike)
  - **Pack Coordination** — multiple Wolf ships share targeting data (-15% player dodge)
  - **Evolutionary Right** — diplomatic encounters can escalate to combat with no reputation penalty
- **Base Ship Stats:** Speed 6 | Armour 7 | Firepower 8 | Crystal Capacity 4 | Crew 7

## Wider Multiverse Factions

### Fairies

- **ID:** `fairies`
- **Realm:** Fairy Realms
- **Species:** Fairy
- **Ideology:** Knowledge is currency
- **Politics:** Fluid mercantile allegiances
- **Role:** Magical traders and information brokers
- **Personality:** Know more than they let on. Mystical, enigmatic, transactional.
- **Abilities:** Magical espionage, enchanted goods, intelligence networks
- **Gameplay:** Offer intel, rare trades, and unique crystal-related knowledge. High-value but unpredictable allies.

### Knights

- **ID:** `knights`
- **Realm:** Knight Kingdoms
- **Species:** Human (chivalric orders)
- **Ideology:** Order through law
- **Politics:** Feudal hierarchy imposing universal order
- **Role:** Order-seekers from medieval realms
- **Personality:** Honourable, rigid, believe in imposing structure on chaos.
- **Abilities:** Heavy armour, disciplined formations, siege engineering
- **Gameplay:** Military alliance at cost of autonomy. Strong combat support but political strings attached.

### Goblins

- **ID:** `goblins`
- **Realm:** Goblin Warrens
- **Species:** Goblin
- **Ideology:** Profit above all
- **Politics:** Anarchic guild cartels
- **Role:** Scavengers, engineers, black-market dealers
- **Personality:** Resourceful, amoral, profit-driven. Will deal with anyone.
- **Abilities:** Rapid ship modification, black-market crystal trade, sabotage services
- **Gameplay:** Ship upgrades, black-market crystal trades, illicit goods. Useful but unreliable.

### Aliens & Space Races

- **Species:** Various
- **Ideology:** Varies wildly by race
- **Politics:** Varies wildly by race

## Conquest & Competition

Every faction is actively competing to become the dominant power across all realms. The goal for every faction is conquest and total control of the multiverse. Aristotle sits at the centre because whoever controls the crystals controls who can participate in that race.

**Mixed-Universe Crews:** Ship crews are NOT faction-pure. Any captain's crew can contain members from across the multiverse. A cat captain can have goblin engineers, fairy scouts, and a knight navigator. Crew composition affects ship capabilities, encounter options, and morale dynamics.
